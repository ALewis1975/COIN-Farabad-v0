  - Source: `functions/core/fn_taskBuildDescription.sqf`, line 409
  - Repeats ~884 times between `13:04:00` and `13:05:13`

## Root cause
- `intelLog` can contain entries that are not the expected full tuple shape.
- The loop in `fn_taskBuildDescription.sqf` accessed `_cat` immediately after `params` unpacking, which fails when entries are short/malformed.

## Fix applied
- Added a tuple-shape guard before unpacking:
  - skip entries that are not arrays
  - skip arrays with fewer than 5 elements
- This prevents `_cat` from being read when missing and stops the RPT spam loop.
