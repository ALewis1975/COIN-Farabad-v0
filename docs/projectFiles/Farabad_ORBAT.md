This file is intended to be the **authoritative reference ORBAT** for all Farabad mission-development threads.

---

- Timeframe: **Mid–late 2011**, final phase of Operation New Dawn.
- Model: **Joint Base Balad–style hub**, with a USAF host installation and Army tenant brigades.
- Ground AO owner: **2nd Brigade Combat Team (2BCT), 82nd Airborne Division**.
- Airfield / base owner: **USAF expeditionary wing (hosted installation model)**.

# COIN Farabad AO (2011) — OPFOR & CIV ORBAT

**Version:** 1.0  
**Context:** 2011-era COIN, fictionalized Takistan AO  

**Faction Mods**
- **3CB Factions – Takistan** (CIV / Friendly local forces)  
- **3CB Factions – Middle Eastern Extremists / Insurgents** (OPFOR)

---

## PART I — LOCAL FRIENDLY FORCES (GREENFOR / CIV SUPPORT)

### 1) Islamic Republic of Takistan (IRT) — Host-Nation Government
Takistan is a fragile but internationally recognized state. Its security forces exist, but effectiveness varies sharply by unit, leadership, and region.

---

### 1.1 Takistan Ministry of Interior (MoI)
Responsible for internal security, policing, and population control.

---

### 2) Takistan National Police (TNP)
- **Faction:** `UK3CB_TKP_I` (Takistan Police variants)  
- **Status:** Friendly / Host Nation  
- **Callsign root:** `TNP` (or `BAZ` for local-language flavor)

**Mission role**
- Routine law enforcement  
- Traffic control and checkpoints  
- Detention and evidence handling  
- Partnered patrols with BLUFOR  
- Population engagement (sometimes coercive)

#### ORBAT (AO-level, notional)

**TNP Provincial Police Headquarters (Farabad Province)**
- TNP HQ FARABAD  
- Police Chief (Colonel-equivalent)  
- Admin / logistics cell  
- Liaison officers (BLUFOR, Army, Civil Affairs)

**TNP District Police Stations**
- TNP DISTRICT NORTH  
- TNP DISTRICT CENTRAL  
- TNP DISTRICT SOUTH  

Each district station controls:
- 2–4 Patrol Sections  
- 1 Checkpoint / Traffic Section  
- 1 Detainee Holding Cell (variable quality)

**TNP Patrol Section**
- Section Chief  
- 6–10 police officers  
- Light weapons (AK-pattern, pistols)  
- Pickups / light trucks  

**TNP Checkpoint Section**
- 4–8 officers  
- Static roadblock / gate  
- Often compromised, intimidated, or co-opted  

**COIN realism note:**  
TNP reliability ranges from professional but undertrained to corrupt or intimidated. This provides narrative flexibility without inventing new factions.

---

### 3) Takistan National Army (TNA) — Limited Presence
- **Faction:** `UK3CB_TKA_I`  
- **Status:** Friendly / Host Nation  
- **Role:** External defense; internal backup when conditions deteriorate

**AO usage guidance**
- Small company-minus presence  
- Poor mobility and logistics  
- Often used as:
  - QRF for major unrest  
  - Perimeter security for key infrastructure  
  - Partnered presence to legitimize operations  

**Notional AO Element**
- TNA Infantry Company (-)  
  - Company HQ  
  - 2–3 Rifle Platoons  
  - Light weapons only  

---

## PART II — CIVILIAN POPULATION (WHITE)

### 4) Civilian Population (Farabad AO)
- **Faction:** `UK3CB_TKC_C`  
- **Status:** Non-combatant, but not neutral  

**Civilian sub-groups (conceptual, not armed factions)**

**Urban Civilians**
- Shopkeepers, drivers, laborers  
- High exposure to police and insurgent pressure  

**Rural / Tribal Communities**
- Village elders (Shura)  
- Often hedging between government and insurgents  

**IDPs / Displaced Persons**
- High grievance potential  
- Recruitment pool for insurgents  

**Economic Actors**
- Truck drivers  
- Fuel smugglers  
- Market brokers  

**Gameplay note:**  
Civilians are the terrain in COIN. Most intelligence flows through them indirectly.

---

## PART III — OPFOR (RED)

### 5) Takistan Islamic Movement (TIM) — Insurgent Network
- **Faction:** `UK3CB_MEI_I`  
- **Status:** Hostile  
- **Callsign root:** `ALPHA` (or `JIHAD`, `HILAL`, etc.)

TIM is a decentralized insurgent movement, not a standing army.

---

### 5.1 TIM Strategic Layer
Invisible to players, but shapes the AO.
- Shura Council (regional)  
- Religious leadership  
- External facilitators (funding, propaganda)  
- Cross-border support (implied)  

---

### 5.2 TIM Operational Structure (AO-level)

#### A) Regional Command — Farabad Wilayat
- Regional Emir  
- Operations cell  
- Finance / logistics cell  
- IED facilitation cell  

#### B) Insurgent Cells (Primary Gameplay Units)

**1) IED Cell**
- Cell Leader  
- 2–4 specialists  
- Functions:
  - IED emplacement  
  - VBIED preparation  
  - Pressure-plate and command detonation  

**2) Guerrilla Attack Cell**
- 6–12 fighters  
- Small arms + RPGs  
- Conducts:
  - Ambushes  
  - Harassment attacks  
  - Hit-and-run raids  

**3) Urban Support Cell**
- Couriers  
- Lookouts  
- Safe-house operators  
- Blends with civilian population  

---

### 5.3 TIM Tactical Elements

**Rural Fighter Group**
- AK-pattern rifles  
- PKM / RPD (rare)  
- RPG-7  
- Technicals (sporadic)  

**Urban Militant Group**
- Smaller teams  
- Concealed weapons  
- IED-trigger support  

**Specialized Threats**
- Sniper cell (rare, high impact)  
- VBIED cell (low frequency, high consequence)

## PART VII — Mission Design Guidance
- Do not spawn OPFOR as a standing force; treat them as event-driven.  
- Use TNP as presence, not combat power.  
- Let civilians act as information carriers, not objectives.  
- Scale violence based on BLUFOR behavior and time-on-station.
